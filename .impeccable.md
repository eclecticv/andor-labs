# Impeccable — project design context

> This project implements the **And/or Labs Design System**
> (`~/Developer/andor-design-system`, a Claude Design project), which is still
> **work in progress**. Evolve it deliberately: extend or reshape tokens and
> `ds/*` components when the work calls for it — don't treat the current system as
> frozen. What stays fixed are the **brand invariants** below (the three type voices,
> the monotone + single-blue palette, no dark mode, the diagram/figure language).
> The impeccable font/color/theme defaults still do NOT apply here — the brand
> fonts and palette override them.

## Design Context

### Users
Founders and GTM/operator leaders at seed→Series-A B2B **adtech** startups,
evaluating And/or Labs as their go-to-market partner. Technical-adjacent,
time-poor, skeptical. They land to answer one question: *"Can this person actually
win us our category?"*

### Brand personality
Plainspoken, technical, dryly confident — an engineer's field notes, not agency
marketing. "We"/"you", every claim carries a number, dry wit used sparingly. No
emoji, no exclamations, no hype words. Mono eyebrows are UPPERCASE and tracked;
the **slash is the brand glyph** (`and / or / not`).

### Aesthetic direction — retro-technical / editorial (makingsoftware.com lineage)
- **Three type voices, no sans.** **Instrument Serif** for *headlines* (display, 400
  only — its thick/thin contrast reads bold; never faux-bold it); **Newsreader** serif
  for *body & reading* (subheads, prose, quotes, names); **Departure Mono** for
  everything that *labels or measures* (eyebrows, captions, UI chrome, `FIG.` numbers,
  buttons, inputs, metadata).
  → Newsreader is on impeccable's reject list; **ignore that here — it is the brand.**
- **Monotone + one signal.** Near-black ink ramp on **pure white**; technical blue
  `#1B4DFF` is the ONLY accent. No gradients, no gradient text, no warm tints,
  **no dark sections / no dark mode.**
- **The diagram language is the brand.** The one grid is an extremely fine **dot**
  pattern. Frame content like manual figures: corner crop ticks, `FIG. NN` labels,
  `+` registration marks, crosshairs, and **dotted-line dividers**.
- **Printed materiality.** Hard offset **block** shadows (never soft glow),
  near-square corners (radii cap ~5px), quick mechanical motion (no bounce/elastic),
  secondary buttons press into their shadow. The signature link is blue with a
  hand-drawn **wavy** underline.

### Design tokens (authoritative — do not substitute)
Live in `src/styles/tokens/*` and mirrored from the DS:
- Ink ramp `--ink-900…--ink-050`, `--paper`/`--paper-pure` (#FFFFFF), `--surface-card` (#F7F9FB).
- Blue `--blue-700…--blue-050`, signal `--blue-500` (#1B4DFF).
- Type: `--font-display` (Instrument Serif, headlines), `--font-serif` (Newsreader,
  body), `--font-mono` (Departure Mono); `.aol-eyebrow`, `.aol-display`. Motion:
  `--dur-*`, `--ease-out`. Shadows: `--shadow-hard*`.
- Grid surfaces: `.aol-grid`, `.aol-grid-blue`. Components: `src/components/ds/*`.

### Design principles (guide every change)
1. **Evolve the system deliberately.** Prefer reusing existing tokens and `ds/*`
   components, but extend, add to, or reshape them when the work calls for it — the
   system is WIP, not frozen. Hold the brand invariants: no new font, no color
   outside the ink ramp + single blue, no gradient, no dark surface.
2. **Everything reads as a technical figure.** Prefer the DS motifs (dotted dividers,
   crop ticks, `FIG.` framing, dot-grid) over generic card/shadow chrome.
3. **Three voices, strict.** Instrument Serif to headline, Newsreader to read, mono to
   label. No fourth typeface, no sans.
4. **Accessibility is non-negotiable.** Text meets WCAG AA (≥4.5:1); motion honors
   `prefers-reduced-motion`; interactive surfaces are keyboard-complete.
5. **Quiet confidence.** Restraint over decoration; the blue accent earns its power
   by being rare (≈10% weight).

### Anti-references
Generic SaaS template (purple/blue gradients, icon-card grids, gradient text),
dark-mode dashboard, startup-cute/mascots, glossy/glassmorphism. The retired "Dub"
navy+prism identity and the interim Space Grotesk + IBM Plex Mono are dead — do not
reintroduce.
